Bonus Distribution Rules

Bonus will be distributed via email several days after the experiment.

Version 1.0 2025-08-21

Overview

All participants receive a guaranteed base payment. Additional bonus depends on both accuracy and completion time, encouraging careful yet efficient work.

Rules

  1. 1
    Base Payment: Everyone receives $2.00 after valid completion.
  2. 2
    Bonus Pool: Up to $3.00 additional bonus is available per person.
  3. 3
    Calculation: Bonus scales with accuracy and speed using the formula below and the examples that follow.

Formula

bonus = $3 × (accuracy_rate) × time_factor

where time_factor = max(0, 1 − (actual_time − target_time) / grace_window).

Click here for some exmaples

Example A — 95% accuracy, within target time：

bonus = 3 × 0.95 × 1.00 = $2.85 → total pay = $2 + $2.85 = $4.85

Example B — 80% accuracy, 5 min over, grace window 10 min：

time_factor = 1 − 5/10 = 0.5 → bonus = 3 × 0.8 × 0.5 = $1.20

Notes

  • Bonuses are rounded to the nearest cent.
  • Submissions must meet quality standards (no spam/empty answers).
  • Payments are typically processed within 5-7 business days.
